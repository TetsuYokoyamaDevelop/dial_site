Dial In - Focus Timer

How to use

Drag the dial to set your focus duration (up to 60 minutes). Tap Start to begin. The green area shrinks as time passes. Tap Pause to stop, or Reset to start over.

Frequently asked questions

Does the screen stay on during the timer?
Yes. The screen stays awake while the timer is running so you can always see your remaining time.

Can I change the color?
Yes. Tap the color circle in the top right corner to choose from 8 theme colors.

Does the timer work in the background?
Yes. When the timer finishes, you'll receive a notification even if the app is in the background or your screen is locked.

Does the app collect any data?
No. See our Privacy Policy for details.
